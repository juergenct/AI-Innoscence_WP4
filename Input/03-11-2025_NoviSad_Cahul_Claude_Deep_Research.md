## KEY INSIGHTS BY ENTITY TYPE COVERAGE

### Excellent Coverage (10,000+ entities):
- **Novi Sad**: Serbian Business Registers Agency (100,000s), GlobalGiving NGOs (48,989), University of Novi Sad (41,000+ students)
- **Cahul**: IDNO.MD Companies (215,000+), GlobalGiving NGOs (13,437), ODA/ODIMM SMEs (55,000+ registered)

### Circular Economy-Specific Sources:
- **Novi Sad**: UNDP Circular Communities Platform, Digital Platform for Circular Economy, BioSense Institute (AgTech/sustainable agriculture)
- **Cahul**: E-Circular Training Center, EU4Moldova Focal Regions Greening SMEs program, UNDP Green Transition projects

### Highest Scrapability (APIs/Structured Downloads):
- Both cities: GlobalGiving Atlas (JSON/CSV API)
- Novi Sad: Serbian Business Registers (NexU-APR searchable database), Crunchbase (API with subscription)
- Cahul: IDNO.MD (structured database), Startup Moldova (ecosystem data), EU4Moldova (published lists)

### Regional Focus for Cahul:
Most sources are national-level Moldovan databases **filterable by Cahul region/district**. City-specific sources include: Cahul Municipality website, Cahul CCI branch, B.P. Hasdeu University, Cahul Business Incubator, Ziuadeazi.md news, and EU4Moldova Focal Regions program which specifically targets Cahul with €23M budget.

### Language Accessibility:
- **Novi Sad**: All major sources available in Serbian (Cyrillic/Latin) and English
- **Cahul**: All major sources available in Romanian; most also offer Russian and/or English versions
